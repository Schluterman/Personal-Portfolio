Arkansas Great War Letter Project

This project originated back in 2016 during my junior year of highschool, During that time I had recently been introduced to web-design and was taking a class to learn. While I was attending this class I was introduced to the Cabot-Arkansas museum director that started this Non-profit transcribing letters for Arkansans's that servied in the great war. The project needed to be hosted and easy enough for the director to edit and add new content, so the project at the time was build and published with Weebly.com. As I continued my education in college this site's content has been my filler content to progress my skills in new design styles and coding experience. The images to your left are my latest wireframe styled for this project. Links have been attached to view the original site I made back in 2016 and another that I created as a mock-up redesign back in 2020.
